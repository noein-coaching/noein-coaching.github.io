Coaching with noein

A calm space to think, reflect, and move forward with clarity.

Transformative coaching offers you time and space to explore what matters, understand what’s shaping your choices, and reconnect with yourself.

Who coaching is for.

I work with individuals and professionals who feel drawn to pause, reflect, and realign — often during moments of transition or uncertainty.

What coaching can help with.

  • Gaining clarity on direction or next steps
  • Feeling stuck or uncertain
  • Reconnecting with values and priorities
  • Building confidence in decision-making
  • Navigating change or transition
  • Understanding unhelpful thought patterns
  • Creating more calm and alignment

How our sessions work.

  • One-to-one transformative coaching
  • 60-minute sessions
  • Online – working with clients worldwide
  • Sessions in English and Greek
  • Mostly evenings and weekends
  • Introductory rate: £50 per session

We begin with a free 30-minute Discovery Call — a relaxed conversation to explore what you’d like to work on and whether coaching feels right for you.
