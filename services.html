Coaching with noein.

A space to think. A space to understand. A space to become.

I offer one-to-one, 60-minute transformative coaching sessions, creating a calm, supportive and collaborative space for you to reflect and explore what matters most.

Transformative coaching offers you time and space to explore what matters, understand what’s shaping your choices and reconnect with your true self.

Coaching at noein is for individuals and professionals who want to understand themselves more deeply and make choices that feel aligned rather than imposed.

How our sessions work.

  • One-to-one 60-minute coaching sessions
  • Available online or in person
  • Sessions in English and Greek
  • Mostly evenings & weekends
  • Payment discussed in discovery call, to suit client needs.

We begin with a free 30-minute Discovery Call — a relaxed conversation to explore what you’d like to work on and whether coaching feels right for you.

Who I work with.

At noein, I support individuals and professionals who, like me, feel a pull toward something more aligned, a desire to reconnect with themselves, gain direction and build confidence in the choices ahead.

My coaching style.

My approach is reflective, calm, and centred around awareness. I meet clients where they are, following their pace, their questions and what naturally arises in the space we create together.

When we understand the thoughts, patterns, and assumptions shaping our lives, we begin to act with greater clarity and intention.

What coaching can help with.

  • Gain clarity on direction or next steps
  • Explore a sense of feeling stuck or off-track
  • Reconnect with values and priorities
  • Build confidence in your own judgement
  • Navigate change or transition
  • Understand unhelpful thought and behaviour patterns
  • Create more calm and alignment

Ready to begin?

If you’re ready for a calm, thoughtful space to reconnect with yourself, you’re welcome to book a discovery call.
