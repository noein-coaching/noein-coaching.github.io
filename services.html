Coaching with noein

A calm space to think, reflect, and move forward with clarity.

Transformative coaching offers you time and space to explore what matters, understand what’s shaping your choices, and remember your true self.

Coaching at noein is for individuals and professionals who want to understand themselves more deeply, and make choices that feel aligned rather than imposed. It’s a space to reconnect with who you are, honour what matters to you, and move forward with greater clarity and confidence.

Who I work with.

At noein, I support individuals and professionals who, like I once did, feel a pull toward something more aligned — a desire to reconnect with themselves, gain direction, and build confidence in the choices ahead.

What coaching can help with.

  • Gaining clarity on direction or next steps
  • Exploring a sense of feeling stuck or off-track
  • Reconnecting with values and priorities
  • Building confidence in your own judgement
  • Navigating change or transition
  • Understanding unhelpful thought and behaviour patterns
  • Creating more calm and alignment

My coaching style.

My approach is reflective, calm, and centred around awareness. I meet clients where they are, following their pace, their questions, and what naturally arises in the space we create together.

When we understand the thoughts, patterns, and assumptions shaping our lives, we begin to act with greater clarity and intention.

How our sessions work.

  • One-to-one transformative coaching
  • 60-minute sessions
  • Online – working with clients worldwide
  • Sessions in English and Greek
  • Mostly evenings and weekends
  • Introductory rate: £50 per session

We begin with a free 30-minute Discovery Call — a relaxed conversation to explore what you’d like to work on and whether coaching feels right for you.
