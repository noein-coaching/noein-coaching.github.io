About Alexandra

Architect of spaces, guide for inner clarity.

I help people who want to reconnect with themselves and move forward with confidence. Trained as an architect and then as a Transformative Coach, I know how it feels to follow what you think you should do until you choose otherwise. Noein is the space I created to help others make that same choice for themselves.

In our work together, you will find thoughtful questions, gentle challenge, and practical structure. It is less about supplying answers and more about making room for the insight that is already there.

Training & background

Where this practice comes from

Before coaching, I designed physical spaces that supported how people live and work. That experience taught me the power of thoughtful structure—now I bring that same care to the inner spaces my clients explore.

I trained as a Transformative Coach, continue to learn alongside peers and mentors, and draw on somatic, architectural, and leadership frameworks to meet each client where they are.

The result is a practice that is practical, spacious, and deeply human.
