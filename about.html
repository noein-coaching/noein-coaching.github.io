I'm Alexandra.

A Transformative Coach, Architect, and the founder of noein.

My journey into coaching began while expanding my own career in architecture. As I grew in that world, I realised the skills that stood out for me were the human ones: listening, guiding, and helping others make sense of their thoughts.

For a long time, I felt pressure to find my place in architecture in a way that felt clearly defined and technical. It was only when I accepted that my interests might take a different form — and that they were still valid, still mine, and could be used meaningfully — that something shifted. With that acceptance came a sense of lightness and confidence, and a release from the stress of trying to force myself into a specialty that didn’t truly reflect who I am.

I began to see that what I had to offer the practice I worked in was exactly this perspective. I introduced a focus on personal development, helping to cultivate a growth culture through mentoring and coaching within the office.

Over time, this understanding became more than an interest; it became a natural path forward. I trained as a Transformative Coach with the Animas Centre for Coaching, realising that I didn’t have to choose between these two sides of myself — I could bring them together.

Noein is the space I once needed myself: a calm place to explore, reflect, and reconnect with what feels true to myself.


A space to think. A space to understand. A space to become.



Introducing coaching in my workplace — nurturing a culture where people can learn and grow.

My philosophy.

Life is often influenced by factors we don’t fully notice. In our busy, demanding, and sometimes stressful lives, it’s easy to lose sight of what truly matters and to find ourselves living in ways that don’t fully reflect our dreams or values.

Coaching helps us pause, notice the choices we’ve made, and understand the reasons behind them. It supports us to reconnect with our values and remember where we want our choices to take us, so we can live with greater intention, clarity, and alignment.

Through this work, I offer calm, reflective transformative coaching online, supporting people who want greater clarity, confidence and alignment in their life or work.
